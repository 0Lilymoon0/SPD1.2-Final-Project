Antonio's Pizzeria

Authentic Italian Pizza with a Slice of Fun!

Safe

Antonio's Pizzeria understands how important a safe space is for an eatery. Each of our fine establishments are cleaned thoroughly each and every day before and after operating hours. We promise no disease will ever come from our establishments.

Healthy

While we are of course a pizzeria, and therefore you might be inclined to expect extremely greasy and unhealthy foods, that is not entirely the case here. Antonio's Pizzeria makes use of the freshest farm-to-table ingredients possible in our dishes, and we make them without all that excess grease.

Fun

Antonio's Pizzeria is more than just a restaurant, it's a place for family. We know that a big part of family is children, and we want them to enjoy themselves as much as possible. Check out our many games where kids can play and earn tickets, to exchange for a neat prize at the prize corner counter!

Come On Down

We've got the best pizza around, we're great with the kids, and we even do birthday parties! What more can you ask for? We hope to see you soon at Antonio's Pizzeria. Until then, take care, and stay hungry!
